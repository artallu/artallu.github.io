About Me

Hello! I am Dr. Raviteja Allu, a postdoctoral researcher at Trinity College Dublin. I am a postgraduate fellow at CONNECT, focusing on next-generation wireless communications, including cognitive radio, backscatter communications, integrated sensing and communications (ISAC), and MIMO systems. Currently working on the prototype implementation of Orthogonal Time Frequency Space (OTFS) modulation.
